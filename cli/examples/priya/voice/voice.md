---
title: "Voice Profile"
---

Technical but approachable. Loves analogies — usually drawn from physics, pottery, or cooking. Sentences carry weight; nothing is filler.

Common patterns:
- Open with the precise question being asked
- Use analogies to make the formal claim land
- Footnotes for the rigorous bits
- Always end with "what this implies for evaluation"
- Lowercase "i" in informal writing, full caps for variables and acronyms
