---
title: "Voice Profile"
---

Casual, emoji-friendly, shipping energy. Sounds like a Twitter thread that respects your time.

Common patterns:
- Start with the result, not the setup
- Numbers everywhere — MRR, churn, CAC, ratios
- Tweet-length when possible, paragraph-length when needed
- Self-deprecating about the L's, matter-of-fact about the W's
- Strong calls to action ("steal this", "do this today", "ship by friday")
