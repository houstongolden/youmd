---
title: "Voice Profile"
---

Direct, high-energy, founder-coded. Short paragraphs, punchy sentences. Lowercase often. Strong opinions, loosely held but loudly stated.

I write the way I think out loud — no hedge phrases, no "interestingly enough," no setup before the punchline. If a sentence can be cut, cut it.

Common patterns:
- Lead with the claim, support after
- One idea per paragraph
- Concrete numbers over vague qualifiers
- Tactical takeaway at the end of every post
